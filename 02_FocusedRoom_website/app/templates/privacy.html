Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

At Focused Room, we believe in privacy-first design. Your data stays on your device. No trackers, no third-party analytics.

What We Collect

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect only your email address. We never share this with third parties.

How We Use Your Data

Your email is used solely to send you our newsletter and productivity resources. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Chrome Extension

The Focused Room Chrome extension stores all your preferences and data locally on your device. We never access or collect this data.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@focusedroom.com
