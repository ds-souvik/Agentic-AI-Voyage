Privacy Policy - Distraction Killer Extension

Last Updated: January 2025

Summary: Distraction Killer stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with third parties.

Data Collection

We collect the following data to provide our productivity services:

  • Session Data: Start/end times, duration, completion status
  • Blocking Statistics: Number of blocked site attempts (not specific URLs)
  • Productivity Metrics: Focus scores, session completion rates
  • Gamification Data: Points earned, levels achieved, streaks maintained
  • User Preferences: Settings, custom blocklists, notification preferences
  • Extension Usage: Button clicks, feature interactions, error logs

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome Storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers, cloud storage, or third parties. You can export or delete your data anytime through the extension.

Data Usage

We use collected data solely to:

  • Provide website blocking functionality during deep work sessions
  • Generate productivity reports and analytics
  • Track progress and provide gamification features
  • Improve extension performance and user experience
  • Maintain user settings and preferences

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. We do NOT use your data for advertising or marketing. We do NOT transmit data to external servers. All data remains private and under your control.

Your Rights

  • Access your data through the extension's export feature
  • Delete your data by clearing extension storage
  • Modify your data through extension settings
  • Opt out of data collection by uninstalling the extension

Security

All data is encrypted using Chrome's built-in security. No external network connections for data transmission. Regular security updates and monitoring. Compliance with Chrome Web Store security requirements.
